are better, sharper, and more developed.

instrument.

FOUNDATIONAL SKILLS
A  prefix   is a word part added to the beginning of 
a base word that changes the meaning of a word. 
The prefix      un-   means not. When          un-   is added to a 
base word, the new word has the opposite 
meaning.
When    the letter     r follows a vowel       , it changes the 
vowel sound. The letters            er, ir, ur,    and   or  can stand 
for the /ûr/ sound, as in purr. Practice with the 
words:
STIR   SPUR   WORD   HERD
GRAMMAR
To  compare two nouns          using an adjective, add            –er  to 
most adjectives that have one syllable. For adjectives with 
one syllable that end in a single vowel followed by a 
consonant, double the last consonant and then add                         –er . 
For adjectives that have two syllables and end in                       y,  
replace the      y  with   i and add     –er . Add   more    before 
adjectives that have two or more syllables and do not                          end 
in  y . 
To  compare more than two nouns              , add   –est  to most 
adjectives that have one syllable. For adjectives that have 
one syllable and end in a single vowel followed by a 
consonant, first double the last consonant and then add 
–est . For adjectives with two syllables that end in                     y , 
replace the      y  with an     i and then add        –est . For adjectives 
that do not end in         y  and have two or more syllables, add 
the word     most    before the adjectives.
A  thesaurus     is a book of synonyms or words with similar 
meanings. It also includes antonyms or words with 
opposite meanings, as well as related words and 
concepts.

Estimating
An  estimate  is a thoughtful  guess , NOT  an  exact answer .
To  estimate , you 
ROUND  FIRST , and 
then  add or subtract .
KEY  WORD  :
ABOUT
For example :
The EXACT  answer  to 
    64+23 = 88
To  estimate , ROUND  65 
to 70. Round  23 to 20.
Now  I can easily  add:
     70+20 = 90
The BEST  estimate  is 
90!
